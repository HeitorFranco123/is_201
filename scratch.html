I love gaming. Why?

  1. Because these games exist:

    • The Witcher 3: Amazing story and world.
    • Dark Souls: Challenging but rewarding.
    • Red Dead Redemption 2: Incredible open world and narrative.
    • Super Mario Odyssey: Just pure, classic fun


  2. Gaming is about commuity! Some of my best friends I met online. This is how:

    • Online Multiplayer Games: Playing games like "Fortnite," "Call of Duty," or "League of Legends" online is a great way to connect with people. You often need to work as a team, which naturally leads to conversations and friendships.
    • Gaming Forums and Communities: Websites like Reddit, Discord servers, or specific game forums are great for meeting people who share your interest in a particular game. You can discuss strategies, share experiences, and plan gaming sessions.
    • Gaming Events and Tournaments: If you're into competitive gaming, participating in or attending gaming tournaments can be a fantastic way to meet fellow gamers in person.
